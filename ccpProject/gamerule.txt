\( 0 W 0 )/ Game Rules
========================================================
1. select the available option between
   Character and Weapon Banner.
2. choose how many pulls you want (1 pull or 10 pull).
3. You can pull again as long you have enough primo
   or end the game.

If you want more primogems you can add more in
"more primo" selection and add more primogems
as much as you want.
